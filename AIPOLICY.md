## AI Usage Policy & Project Status Disclosure

I am a hobby dev and just getting started learning in my off hours and free time. I am using Agentic AI tools to speed up certain parts of the process (various harnesses using a combination of Claude, Codex and Various Locally Run llms).

So, just to be clear, AI isn't meant to replace the review process that humans do. Instead, anything that's created with AI still gets looked over, edited, and tested before it's actually used, whenever that's possible. There might be some areas that haven't been thoroughly reviewed from a technical standpoint yet or areas I may not have missed major issues. This is another reason I have decided to make this project open source, feedback is important to me I want to make it better but it may take time. If there is enough interest I may entertain PRs in the future but at this point I am still attempting to make the project more stable.

A few things to keep in mind:

- **Human oversight is part of the process:** AI may help draft or suggest content, but final decisions, direction and implementation choices are made by a human.
- **Expect some gaps:** Documentation may be incomplete, outdated or missing details while the project is actively evolving.
- **Mistakes can happen:** Since this project is still being worked on, you might come across some unexpected issues, like things not working right or features that are not finished yet.
- **Things might change:** Right now, I've got a few projects going on at the same time, so I'll be working on this one in bits and pieces, rather than following a set plan. This means progress might be a little uneven, with some busy periods followed by quieter ones. My own time constraints will also come into play to affect the speed of development.  

As the project continues to grow and develop, I'm constantly looking for ways to make it better.

Feedback, bug reports, questions, and suggestions are very welcome. If something looks wrong, please feel free to open an issue or start a discussion or join my discord server https://discord.gg/WyDmWdThXM
